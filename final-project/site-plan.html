Feed The Nation – Website Project Plan

Slogan: “The Gospel is True”

Site Name

Feed The Nation (FTN)

This name was selected because it perfectly reflects the purpose of the website: assisting individuals and families with food access, education, medical care, career opportunities, child support resources, and community upliftment.

Site Purpose

Feed The Nation is designed to be a resource hub where users can find help with food, education, medical assistance, job opportunities, and skill development. The website also provides pathways for volunteers and donors to offer support through opportunities, donations, and services. Users may also complete surveys to help the organization understand their needs and provide the appropriate assistance.

Scenarios

  • “Where can I find food assistance or community food programs near me?”
  • “How can I donate money, food, or job opportunities to help people in need?”
  • “Where can I request help with education, medical care, or job training?”
  • “How do I take the FTN survey so I can receive support?”

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Dark Green (#1a5e3f)

Used for: Headings, accents, buttons, emphasis text

Secondary Color: Warm Yellow (#f2c94c)

Used for: Highlights, call-to-action elements

Background Color: Light Gray/White (#f8f8f8)

Used for: General site background

Text Color: Very Dark Gray (#222)

Used for: Body text for readability

Typography

Font 1: Georgia, serif — Used for all main headings

Font 2: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif — Used for paragraphs and navigation text

These fonts were chosen because they provide strong readability, clean hierarchy, and accessibility.
