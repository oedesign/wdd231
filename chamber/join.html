NP Membership — benefits (no fee)

Cost: None (proof of non-profit status required)

  • Listing in our partners & resources directory
  • One complimentary event listing per quarter
  • Access to member-only newsletters and community forums

Bronze Membership — benefits

Cost: Affordable entry-level

  • Business listing and category placement
  • Monthly newsletter feature rotation
  • Discounted ticket pricing to local events

Silver Membership — benefits

Cost: Mid-tier

  • Priority listing and rotating spotlight on homepage
  • Free/discounted workshops and training sessions
  • Enhanced event promotion and social media shoutouts

Gold Membership — benefits

Cost: Premium

  • Featured advertising/spotlight on homepage
  • VIP invitations and speaker opportunities at major events
  • Dedicated promotion and priority support from the chamber team
